About

I am a postdoctoral teaching fellow working under Dr. Joel Schildbach at Johns Hopkins University. Currently, I am collaborating with Dr. Frederick Tan at the Carnegie Institution Department of Embryology to develop curricula and online tutorials teaching bioinformatics. I am excited about using technology to enhance education, and want to use C-MOOR to help my students learn how real scientific research is being done today and participate in discovering the secrets of biology using data analysis.
