About

I am a PhD candidate in Dr. Mark Van Doren’s lab at Johns Hopkins University. The focus of my research is on how post-transcriptional gene regulation influences fertility, and relies heavily on bioinformatics. I’m passionate about undergraduate education, and excited by how C-MOOR can be used to give students the opportunity to learn bioinformatics, and analyze data that’s part of ongoing research.
