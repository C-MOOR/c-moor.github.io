About

I am an Instructor at Clovis Community College and the former Director of C-MOOR. I am using the C-MOOR platform in our Biology for Majors course, BIOL11A. In addition, C-MOOR will be a central component of our new course, Research in Biology, BIOL12. These courses provide our students with authentic data science research opportunities and prepare them for rigorous upper-level courses after transfer. My goal is to narrow the STEM gap for minority and first-generation college students, and to provide all students with an authentic research experience.
